Working Process

From Raw Steel to Flawless Finish

01

Sandblasting

Steel-grit blasting to Sa 2.5 cleanliness with 50–75 μm anchor profile. Removes all rust, scale, and contaminants.

02

Pre-Treatment

Solvent cleaning, phosphate conversion, rinsing, and oven drying — documented at every step before coating.

03

Powder Application

Electrostatic spray of epoxy, polyester, or hybrid powders for uniform, consistent coverage on all geometries.

04

Oven Curing

Heat-cured in precision ovens — powder melts and cross-links into a tough, seamless protective film.

05

QC Inspection

Dry-film thickness gauge (ASTM D7091), adhesion cross-cut (D3359), and salt-spray validation (B117).
