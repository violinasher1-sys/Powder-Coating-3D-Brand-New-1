FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum component size you can coat?

Our oven and handling system accommodates components up to 6.5 × 1.7 × 3 metres. For oversized items we offer on-site industrial painting as an alternative.

What sandblasting standard do you work to?

We blast to Sa 2.5 (ISO 8501-1) with a 50–75 μm anchor profile as standard. Sa 3 white-metal blast is available on request for aggressive environments.

Are you approved for ADDC / TAQA projects?

Yes. Powder Coating® General Contracting LLC is an approved applicator for TAQA (Abu Dhabi Distribution Company) and holds Civil Defence approval, making us compliant for utility and infrastructure projects across Abu Dhabi.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 3–5 working days depending on quantity and coating system. Rush jobs can be accommodated — contact us to discuss your schedule.

Do you provide QC documentation?

Yes. Every batch is accompanied by dry-film thickness readings (ASTM D7091), adhesion test results (D3359), and salt-spray reports (B117) on request. Full batch traceability is standard.
