About Us

Committed to Excellence in Powder Coating®

Powder Coating® General Contracting LLC is a leading industrial coating, sandblasting, and painting company based at I-CAD Industrial Area, Musaffah, Abu Dhabi — approved by TAQA (ADDC) and Civil Defence.

Founded with a simple vision — to deliver superior coating solutions with unwavering commitment to quality — we proudly serve power & utility, oil & gas, infrastructure, and steel fabrication clients across the UAE.

We are a certified applicator for National Paints PE & PA 1000 Series, reinforcing our commitment to recognized industry standards. Every project is backed by documented quality control and ASTM-aligned testing.

To deliver exceptional coating services that enhance the aesthetics and longevity of your steel assets — on time, on spec.

Our Vision

To be the UAE's most trusted industrial coating partner — known for precision, reliability, and documented excellence.

Our Process

1

Consultation & Specification

Understanding substrate, corrosion category, color, and schedule requirements.

2

Sandblasting to Sa 2.5

Steel-grit blasting with 50–75 μm anchor profile in our 24 m² blast booth.

3

Chemical Pre-Treatment

Phosphate conversion, rinse, and oven drying — documented stage by stage.

4

Powder Application

Electrostatic spray of epoxy, polyester, or hybrid powder — even coverage on all geometries.

5

Curing & Inspection

Heat cure + DFT gauge, cross-cut adhesion, and salt-spray testing before delivery.
